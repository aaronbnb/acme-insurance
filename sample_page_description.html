TPG Sample Page Test Page Description

Visually, the sample page contains the following sections and content:

Navigation menu

A list of five items:

  1. About us
  2. Our goals and vision
  3. Portfolio
  4. FAQs
  5. Contact

The second item is visually styled to represent the current page.

Jumbotron

A large area near the top of the page, contaning the following:

  1. A large heading with the text "Our goals and Vision".
  2. A quote by the company founder, Mark Smith.
  3. A Stock photo background of a smiling woman.

Find out what role you can play in our corporate family

This section contains the following:

  1. A medium sized heading, with the text "Find out what role you can play in our corporate family".
  2. A tablist with three tabs: "Client", "Saff", and "Contractor". The individual tab panel contents are described below.

Client tab

The tab panel contains the following:

  1. A small heading, with the text "Client".
  2. Styled text and icons: "Our approach to solving your problems: Problems + Us = Solutions".

Staff tab

The tab panel contains the following:

  1. A small heading, with the text "Staff".
  2. A table with four columns:
    • The column headers are "Function", "Salary", "Full Time", "Contact".
    • The first, third and fourth cells in the "Full Time" column contain green checkmark icons.
    • The "contact" column contains linked names.
    • The text above the table reads "Current job vacancies:"

Contractor tab

The tab panel contains the following:

  1. A small heading, with the text "Contractors".
  2. Three paragraphs of text.
  3. An image of a business man folding his arms, and the text "Mark Smith, Founder and CEO".
  4. The image has a custom tooltip that appears on hover and focus. The tooltip text reads:
    Objectively innovate empowered manufactured products whereas parallel platforms. Holisticly predominate extensible testing procedures for reliable supply chains. Carousel

An automatically updating animated carousel, containing three slides: "Customer satisfaction", "Risk management", and "Client support". Each carousel slide contains a stock photo, a paragraph of text, and a "read more" link.

Our publications

This section contains the following:

  1. A small heading, with the text "Our publications".
  2. Instructional text: "Note: our top rated articles are highlighted like this". The word "this" is styled as bold white text with a blue background.
  3. A list of publication titles and authors. Some of them are styled as bold white text with a blue background.

We'd love to hear from you!

This is a form, that contains the following sections:

Personal details

This section contains three fields: "First name", "Last name", and "Your feedback". The first two fields have a red asterisk to the right of them. The third field has a formatting hint below it. When the form is submitted, invalid fields are indicated through a red border.

Additional details

This section contains the following:

  • A radio button group containing three radio buttons. Above them is a group label with the text "Preferred Contact Method". The radio buttons are labeled "By phone", "By mail", and "By letter".
  • A submit button. When the form is submitted without errors, a toast message appears at the top of the screen for a few seconds. The toast message reads "We will respond within 24 hours. Your ticket number is 43556322". The number at the end is a link.

When the form is submitted without selecting a radio button, red warning text will appear above the radio group urging the user to make a selection. Selecting a radio button dynamically changes the contents of the rest of the form section, described in in more detail below.

By Phone

If the "by phone" option is selected, the following fields will appear below the radio group:

  1. A text field labeled "phone number". The field has a formatting hint below the field.
  2. A select list labeled "phone type".

By Email

If the "by email" option is selected, the following fields will appear below the radio group:

  1. A text field labeled "email address".
  2. A button labeled "send a copy". The button can be toggled on or off, which is visually indicated by a checkmark next to the button label.
  3. A textfield labeled "Email Address to Copy". When the "send a copy" button is not pressed, this field looks faded can cannot receive focus by clicking on it.

By Letter

If the "by letter" option is selected, the following fields will appear below the radio group:

  1. A text field labeled "Street address".
  2. A button labeled "More Address Details". The button functions as a toggle button that shows or hides two extra fields: "zip code" and "city". Toggling the button does not change its visual appearance.

Footer

The page footer is styled with a dark gray background and semi-transparent white text and icons (which appears as a light gray color). The footer contains four columns, each of which contains a small heading and a list of links. The second column contains social media icons in the last list item.

Sticky footer

There is a permanently visible sticky footer that is fixed to the bottom of the screen, regardless of scrolling position. This sticky footer contains an icon of an alarm bell, and the text "Don't miss out! Only 1 week left in our annual sale! Click here to find out more". The word "here" is a link. The background color is aqua, the text color is white, and the link color is yellow.

Responsive behavior

On smaller screens or larger browser zooming levels, the following responsive changes occur:

  • The navigation menu is hidden. Instead, there is a so called "hamburger" menu button (a button icon with three horiontal lines). This button can be used to expand or collapse the navigation menu.
  • Sections that were laid out side by side are not laid out as a single column.
  • Carousel slides no longer show their textual contents, just the stock photos.
